Known Issues

To help you avoid frustration with unexpected issues, we’ve compiled a list of known issues you might experience while using Rohan.

Scrolling Issues

If the cursor is far too distant (say, many many pages away) from the visible area, the app’s automatic scrolling may not bring it fully into view.

This is a known issue affecting multiple macOS developers, and a solution isn’t yet available. If this occurs, simply scroll manually to reveal the cursor position.
